Free Libre and Open Source Podcast player (podcatcher)

<b>About</b>
This is my contribution to the Android ecosystem and podcast lovers. It's a modern, free podcatcher application which does not collect any of your data, asks for creepy permissions or other malicious things.

<b>Selected Features</b>
* All the basic features you except
* Free and open source
* Modern (material) design
* full gPodder support
* chromecast support
* Sonos support
* No creepy permissions or data collection (without your acceptance)
* A really nice developer who would love to hear if you have any problems whatsoever :)
